GLCM parameters: ROI pixel values were quantized to 32 gray levels. Gray-level co-occurrence matrices were computed with a one-pixel offset at four orientations (0°, 45°, 90°, 135°) following Haralick et al. (1973); directional estimates were averaged to obtain rotationally invariant ROI-level descriptors. Statistic definitions: Contrast = Σi,j (i − j)² · p(i,j); Entropy = −Σi,j p(i,j) · log₂ p(i,j); Homogeneity = Σi,j p(i,j) / (1 + |i − j|), where p(i,j) is the normalized co-occurrence frequency for gray levels i and j.
